Let's talk Pyxis

May 2023 | Andoroid Spyware | Fork on GitHub

Pyxis open source is an Android spyware application designed to help parents monitor their children's activities on their smartphones. It offers a range of features that enable parents to keep a watchful eye on their child's online and offline interactions, ensuring their safety and well-being. Please note that the use of such software should adhere to legal and ethical guidelines, and it's important to respect privacy rights.

Features

— Sent/Received SMS
— Take pictures from front and back camera
— Keylogger
— Read Notifications
— Phishing Social Network

1. Multiple Child Clients Pyxis allows parents to monitor multiple child devices simultaneously. This feature is particularly useful for parents with multiple children, ensuring comprehensive oversight without limitations.
2. SMS Monitoring Pyxis allows parents to monitor their child's text messages, providing insight into their communication patterns and identifying any potential risks or concerns.
3. Photo Capture Pyxis enables parents to remotely capture photos using the child's device camera. This feature can be valuable in verifying the child's surroundings or identifying potential risks.
4. Keylogger The app includes a keylogger function that records all keystrokes made on the child's device. This feature can help parents monitor their child's online activities, including passwords, messages, and search history.
5. Phishing Social Network Pyxis provides notifications for phishing attempts on popular social networking platforms like WhatsApp, Instagram, and Messenger. This helps parents educate their children about online scams and protects them from potential threats.
